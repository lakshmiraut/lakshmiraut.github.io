I taught graduate and undergraduate economics courses at University of California-San Diego, University of Hawaii-Manoa, Yale University and California State University at Fullerton. I built these course pages when I was teaching prior to joining the US Social Security Administration in 2005.  I built these pages using serverside programming with ASP and a backend database to serve student specific course materials to my students.  You may go through various course pages. You will be able to see only the public materials like syllabus and sometimes homework.   In future when I teach again, I will upgrade these using contemporary  IT technology and  textbooks.  This site  does not have links to the courses I taught at the University of California-San Diego, as the capable IT facilities were not available at that time.
