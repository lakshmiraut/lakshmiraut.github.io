Issues on Social Security for Less Developed Countries

 

  • (1) (What is Social Security?) Broadly, what is a Social Security Program? Describe briefly how social security transfers resources from children to parents. What role does social security play sharing of risks of old-age disability, longevity, uncertain macroeconomic fluctuations of inflation and stock prices?
  • (2) (Informal arrangements of Old Age Pension in LDCs). In the absence of a formal publicly provided social security program, which is the case for many less developed countries, how these transfers are performed informally? Whats wrong with these informal transfers? Why should we have formal social security system?
  • (3) (Types of Social Security) Explain various concepts relating to social security: (a) pay-as-you-go, (b) fully funded, (c) defined contribution, (d) defined benefits.
  • (4) (Source and Nature of Social Security Crisis around the World) Describe it.
  • (5) (Social Security Reforms, Redesigning Social Security Program). The Multipillar system suggested by the World Bank Research Group. Describe it.
